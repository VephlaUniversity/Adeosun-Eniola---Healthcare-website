    The Altus Emergency
    Experience

    Get a sneak peak at what it’s like to visit an Altus Emergency facility. At Altus, we genuinely value our patients’ experience. From the time that you walk into any of our lovely, clean facilities, our local team including warm registers, board certified in medical emergency doctors, ER trained physicians, local ER nurses, ER rad techs will be happy to care for you quickly and efficiently.


    We offer on-site 24/7 cutting edge medical emergency technology for all ages. This includes our state-of-the-art imaging equipment, certified laboratory and pharmacy and even comfy pediatric rooms for the precious little ones. It is our mission to meet and surpass your expectations to ultimately deliver you the best patient care when you need it the most. Come to Altus Emergency and experience the difference.
